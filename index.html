My Favorite Hobby


Bicycle Riding

My favorite hobby is bicycle riding. I get to have fun and excercise while riding my bicycle. It's a great experience to mountain bike on trails, or zoom through cities, or just bike leisurely through the suburbs.

I get to see many beautiful sights when I am on my bicycle, much more than when I'm inside a car. Anywhere I go, it's always a great experience to go on a bike ride.
